Decisions For Dale

Dale says to himself "At my age, dancing is not the best idea. I could get seriously hurt!"

"There must be another way to keep myself occupied" he thought.

As he turned the page, he saw another ad that caught his attention.

It was a classified ad that read "VISIT YOUR LOCAL ANIMAL SHELTER AND ADOPT A PET TODAY"

Adjacent to this ad, he sees another post that reads "SPARE ROBOT PARTS FOR SALE AT LOCAL SCRAP YARD"

Dale thinks to himself "Wow, both of these ads are intriguing... which one should I pursue?"

Dale says "A new pet would be wonderful!".
Dale says "I've always been interested in machines. Maybe I'll find some fun robot parts at the scrap yard."
