Faced With A Decision

"A new pet would a great idea!" Dale thought. Dale leaped into his hatchback and drove 1.5 miles away to the animal shelter from the advertisement.

As soon as he got to the cages, he saw the dog he knew he'd love.

"What's her name" Dale asked the shelter worker. "Her name is Bessie" she responded.

"I'll take her!" Dale said with excitement.

Dale filled out the necessary paperwork and took Bessie home with him the same day.

Since then, Dale has made sure to spend every waking moment with Bessie. They do everything together, from playing frisbee in the park, to quiet walks through the neighborhood.

In the evenings, Bessie lays across Dale's lap as he reads the daily newspaper.

THE END
