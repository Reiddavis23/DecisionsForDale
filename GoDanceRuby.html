Faced With A Decision

"Ruby is that you?"... Dale faintly whispers as he approaches Ruby from behind.

Ruby turns around... "Dale Johnson, I can't believe it! I never thought I'd see you again."

Dale says to Ruby... "Wow, you are as beautiful as the day I met you."

"Oh Dale, you've always been the charmer!" Ruby responds, clearly blushing.

"Ruby, I would love to be your dance partner for the evening, in the event that you don't have one already." Dale says back.

"That would be more than delightful." says Ruby proudly.
